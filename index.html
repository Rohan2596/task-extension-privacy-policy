Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 10, 2025

Overview

This Chrome extension ("the Extension") helps you create, categorise, and manage your tasks directly from the browser toolbar. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains what data we store and how it is used.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information (PII), health data, financial data, authentication credentials, personal communications, location data, browsing history, or website content.

Data Stored

  • Task titles you create
  • Task categories you select
  • Completion status of your tasks

This data is stored locally using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API. If you are signed into Chrome, the data may synchronize across your devices through your Google account. We do not have access to this information.

Data Sharing

No stored data is shared with, sold to, or transferred to third parties.

Permissions

The Extension requires the storage permission solely to store and retrieve your task list. No other permissions are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
